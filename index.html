About

I'm Maylusye ,a Roblox Developer who builds clean, reliable, and modular systems.

I've been learning Roblox development for the past several months, focusing on writing organized and maintainable Luau code.

I enjoy building individual gameplay systems such as shops, NPCs, reward systems, DataStores, and user interfaces. My goal is to create reusable, easy-to-configure systems that can be integrated into any Roblox project.

I value clean code, modular architecture, and clear organization, making my projects easy to understand, maintain, and expand.

Payment

Payment is made in Robux only. By default, commissions are split into milestones, with payment due after each milestone is completed and approved. I also offer a 50/50 option (50% upfront, 50% upon completion) for smaller commissions if preferred. I don't accept revenue share or "pay when the game ships" arrangements.

Robux
Maylusye

Payments can be made via Game Pass or Group Funds. Because Roblox charges a 30% marketplace fee, you'll need to cover that fee so the amount I receive matches the agreed commission price. Robux prices are calculated using the current DevEx conversion rate.

Ownership

Once a milestone has been paid for, ownership of that work transfers to you. You're free to modify, publish, or use it however you'd like. I don't resell or reuse commissioned code for other clients.

Refunds

You may cancel your commission at any time. Milestone payments: You only pay for completed milestones. 50/50 payments: The upfront payment is fully refundable until work begins. Once development has started, refunds are only available for unfinished work.

Availability

Timezone: EST (UTC−5 / UTC−4) I usually respond within a few hours on Discord. Email responses may take longer.

Delivery

Most commissions are completed within 2–14 days, depending on scope and my current queue.
